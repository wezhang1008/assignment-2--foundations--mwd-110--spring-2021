Early Life

Ever since she could remember Breanna Wiggy found her love of art in 8th grade. She choose to study studio art instead of learning the violin during middle. She continued in highschool by enrolling in Dark Room Photography, Painting, Drawing, AP Studio art from then on her talent expanded. Since then her love for art transformed into a passion for life. Bree continues her passion striving to earn awards along the way. She has won awards such as a Gold and Silver Key for her photography. In 2017 she was awarded Best Inshow in the Long Island Public Education system. In the New York State Division, she was awarded Most Creative in Illustration on a piece in response to the poem "Jabberwocky" by Lewis Carol. Her piece comments on our nations president in reference to Carol's poem.

College

Bree currently attends the University of Hartford, enrolled in the Photography program and trying to minor in Art History. She was in an exhibition called "La Mostra" in Cortona Italy. She was also feautred in the freshman exhibition. Bree is also the Vice Preisdent of Phi Mu Fraternity.

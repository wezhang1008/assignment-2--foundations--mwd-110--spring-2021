Basic Information

Khunn Egga Oo (Edgar) is a student attending the University of Hartford. He is attending the second semester of his first year of the Audio Engineering Technology major program. He comes from Yangon, Myanmar, a country in South-East Asia. This is his first year living in America. Edgar has many hobbies, including listening to music, watching and playing tennis and soccer. Edgar focuses on learning about the technical aspect of music in his time at University of Hartford to broaden his knowledge on mixing music and equipment used in a recording studio. Edgar also enjoys spending time with his family and friends during the breaks he gets from school.
